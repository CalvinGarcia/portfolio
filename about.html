About Calvin Garcia

Calvin Garcia is a New York City based graphic designer and illustrator. After being dubbed Sasquatch for his wide gait while in a hurry, Calvin saw it as an opportunity to create a brand. The Sasquatch is elusive, and being a problem solver, Calvin prides himself on solving elusive problems.

In the past he has worked as a warehouse manager which has given him a unique perspective on the importance of systems, organization, and keeping up with a fast pace. While designing Calvin strives for maximum satisfaction of the client, and above all seeks to convey a clear cut message in all of his work.
